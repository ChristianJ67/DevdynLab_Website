Get Involved with Our Research

Participate in Our Studies

We are always looking for participants to take part in our research studies. Whether you're a student, a parent, or a community member, your contribution is invaluable in helping us advance our understanding of developmental dynamics. Our studies often involve observational sessions, interactive tasks, or surveys that explore various aspects of cognitive and behavioral development.

If you are interested in participating, we encourage you to visit our Participate page to see if you are eligible for any of our active studies.

Internships and Volunteer Opportunities

If you are interested in joining our lab as an undergraduate research assistant, please complete this brief survey and email Dr. Drew Abney at drew.abney@uga.edu with the subject line "Research Assistant Interest." In your email, kindly include a brief paragraph outlining your interest in working in the lab. Both the survey and the email are required steps in the application process.

Current Openings

We currently DO NOT have any openings for research assistants, but will be recruiting more for the spring semester. This position involves working closely with our research team to support ongoing projects. Responsibilities may include recruiting participants, conducting experimental sessions, managing data, and contributing to research publications.

Unfortunately we are currently not recruiting any Lab Managers, or Post-docs at this time.
